Presidential bot debate






Created as part of a research project at Stony Brook University to learn how to use language to bridge idealogical divides. As a first step, we try to achieve this goal by generating integratively complex language and surfacing the diverse viewpoints. Researchers: Vijay Teja Gottipati, Shreya Bhatia, Harrison Termotto, Niranjan Balasubramanian, and H. Andrew Schwartz.
